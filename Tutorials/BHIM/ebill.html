Step 1

Click on "pay bills" option in the centre of homepage

Step 2

Click on "Electricity" option

Step 3

Select biller service

Step 4

Enter your consumer number

Step 5

Read the bill shown, and click on "pay"

Step 6

Enter your UPI PIN and click on the tick in the right corner

Step 7

Bill paid successfully, click on "home" option and exit the app
