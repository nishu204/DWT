About our project

As we are moving towards a cashless society, there is an increase in users of fintech applications and online payment systems. Yet many people above age 40 find it difficult to use digital wallet platforms and online payment systems. Here is our project to help those people.

Snapshot of every step with clear Instruction

Take your cursor to tutorials where dropdown menu will appear. Choose the application and then type of transaction you want to perform. You will get series of pictures to assist you during transaction.

Video Tutorials

You will get link to the youtube video at the end of descriptive instruction.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • In which languages videos are available?

    Videos are currently available only in Marathi due to linguistic limitations but we are willing to provide it in multiple Indian languages.

  • How long will it take to learn and do a transaction?

    All the videos are under 2 minute long. After watching video it will take less than 1 min to complete transaction.

  • For which applications tutorials were made?

    Tutorials were made for PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm and BHIM.

  • Which are the types of transaction that we have covered?

    We have covered money tranfer using mobile number, UPI ID, bank account details. We have also covered basic bill payment transactions such as electricity bill payment, dth recharge and mobile recharge.

  • Why should I use this website instead of directly searching video on Youtube?

    Our website provide step by step descriptive as well as a video solution to your problem. We have designed this website in such a way that user will understand solution in short time. Our tutorials are short and provide clear instruction to user. Other videos on Youtube are leanthy and lacks clearity.

  • What if I face any difficulty during transaction?

    If you face any problem you can contact us by writing your contact details in the contact box provided at end of this page.
